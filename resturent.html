GOOD FOOD : GOOD MOOD

Aish restaurant, which lies within Hyderabad’s elegant Park Hotel, pays tribute to its ancient legacy and depicts its regal pomp. Under an elegant carved ceiling, diners could explore the mind-blowing menu of classic Hyderabadi cuisines. Enjoy the contemporary ambiance of the restaurant and enjoy the true flavors of Hyderabad. Cost for two: INR 3,000. Opening time: 12:30 pm to 11:30 pm.
